Hola!

My name is Alvaro Rojas, and I'm from Spain

Up till now I've worked as a Strategy Consultant in Madrid, a Tech & Trade Advisor for the Spanish Embassy in LA and launched a beverage company in US.

I'm also a full stack web developer - I made this web page and other apps like GOSURF!

I love photography, traveling and anything outdoors. You can check out my work on Instagram or through my portfolio . Contact me for free HD image files.

I also tend to blog about stuff here. I'm always looking for new projects and ideas - get in touch!
